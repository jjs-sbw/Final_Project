U.S. Dept. of Defense Systems Engineering and Implications for SE Implementation in Other Domains

Presented at EuSEC 2000

Joseph J. Simpson and Mary J. Simpson

Abstract: This paper presents a high-level, simplified view of systems engineering to estgablish a comon context for discussing systems engineering environments. It examines often unstated bases ans assumptions upon which systems engineering implementation in the U.S. Military is based, and offers observations reguarding factors that deserve consideration in implementation of systems engineering outside of the military domain.
