Innovation and Technology Management

Presented at INCOSE 2002

Joseph J. Simpson

Abstract: A systematic approach to innovation and technology management is outlined in terms of a generic systems engineering process. Two types of innovation, incremental and disruptive, are described and discussed. All aspects of the product development and application environment must be correctly managed if an organization wishes to consistently produce innovative technologies and products. System engineering concepts provides a structured framework for the successful practice of innovation and technology development, deployment and management.
